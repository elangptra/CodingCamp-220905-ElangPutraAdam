Our Profile

Persona 3 Reload is a 2024 role-playing video game developed by Atlus. It is a remake of Persona 3, the fourth entry in the main Persona series, which is also part of the larger Megami Tensei franchise.

Reload brings modern visuals, new mechanics, and improved quality-of-life features while still preserving the original story and the classic atmosphere that made it so memorable.

  • Remake with updated graphics and animations
  • Improved quality-of-life (QoL) features
  • Refreshed yet iconic storyline
  • Part of the Megami Tensei series

Characters
